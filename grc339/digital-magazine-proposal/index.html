Introduction

Upcylced

This magazine is meant to bring attention to upcycled art, fashion, and furniture happening today. Out of a need to reduce our carbon footprint, some artist have turned to untraditional medias, like recycled goods, giving them new meaning. We will be featuring artists making head way in their respective industries, as well as share sustainability practices.
