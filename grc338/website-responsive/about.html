About Us

This service was inspired from a conversation with friends of the founder. Being from a friend group with so many bold personalities and opinions has always made it hard to choose a single restaurant to eat at. The goal of this service is to settle the endless quarrel about where to eat, while still being able to enjoy a meal out with your friends. We hope that you all are happy and satisfied with the services and experiences we can provide to you at a discount rate!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does it work?

    Using your smart device or our in-house tablet, you pre-order food to our location as you would with other mobile delivery services.

  • How do I pay?

    You are charged to your card at the end of ordering. This covers your food and any addition service charges, like delivery fees and tips.

  • Are your delivery rates fair?

    Our runners charge a discounted rate compared to other mobile deliverey services.

  • Is gratuity included?

    Gratuity is included as part of the services charges you pay on our app. You can choose to add additional gratuity.

  • Where are you located?

    We are located at 1234 Grand Avenue, San Luis Obispo, CA 93405. Check our location page for more details.
