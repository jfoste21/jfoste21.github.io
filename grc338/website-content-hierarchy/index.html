Dine and Dash

Order from anywhere and eat together

Have you ever been with friends, but all wanted to eat from different resturants?

This is the place for you! Eat out at differnt places together using your smart phone or our in-store tablets. Using our carrier service, food can be ordered from any local resturant and delivered to our location.

How does it work?

You pre-pay for your meals using a smart device. You will be seated and served your food as it comes to our location. Don't worry about the mess! We'll clean it for you. It's a one stop shop to eat our with your friends when you all want differnt food.
